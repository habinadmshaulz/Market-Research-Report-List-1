<p><p>The PCB solder resist ink market comprises three main types: Photoimageable SRI, UV Curable SRI, and Thermal Curable SRI. Photoimageable SRI uses light exposure to create precise patterns, making it ideal for high-density applications. UV Curable SRI cures instantly when exposed to ultraviolet light, enabling rapid production and efficient workflows. Thermal Curable SRI relies on heat to achieve curing, suitable for applications requiring high thermal stability. These variations cater to diverse manufacturing needs and technological advancements in circuit board production.</p></p>
