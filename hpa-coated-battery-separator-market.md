<p><ul><li>Wet Diaphragm</li><li>Dry Diaphragm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HPA coated battery separator market is primarily divided into wet diaphragm and dry diaphragm types. Wet diaphragm separators involve a coating process that uses liquid materials, enhancing ionic conductivity and thermal stability, making them suitable for high-performance lithium-ion batteries. Conversely, dry diaphragm separators utilize a solid-state coating method, resulting in a thinner profile, improved mechanical strength, and reduced weight. Both types cater to the increasing demand for efficient and durable battery solutions in electric vehicles and energy storage applications.</p></p>
