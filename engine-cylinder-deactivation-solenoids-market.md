<p><p>The engine cylinder deactivation solenoids market is categorized into normally closed and normally open solenoid valves. Normally closed solenoids remain shut when not energized, preventing fluid flow until activated, enhancing control in cylinder deactivation for fuel efficiency. In contrast, normally open solenoids allow fluid flow in a deactivated state, closing when energized, which can simplify certain designs. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing engine performance and emissions, catering to different automotive requirements and operational strategies.</p></p>
