<p><p>The hydrolyzed corn starch market can be segmented into three main types based on viscosity: 100cp, 200cp, and 300cp. These classifications indicate the thickness or flow characteristics of the starch when dissolved in water. The 100cp variant is often used for products requiring low viscosity, such as sauces and dressings. The 200cp type is suitable for baked goods, providing moderate thickness, while the 300cp variant offers a high viscosity, ideal for applications in coatings and adhesives.</p></p>
