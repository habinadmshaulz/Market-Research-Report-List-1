<p><strong>The Exhaust Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Dispersive Infrared</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyser</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exhaust analyzers come in several types, each utilizing distinct technologies for measuring emissions. Non-dispersive infrared (NDIR) analyzers detect gases like CO2 and CO by measuring infrared light absorption. Flame ionization detectors (FID) identify hydrocarbons by ionizing compounds in a flame. Chemiluminescence analyzers measure nitrogen oxides through light emission from chemical reactions. Other types may include mass spectrometers and optical sensors, catering to diverse applications in automotive testing, environmental monitoring, and industrial compliance. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring emission standards are met.</p></p>
