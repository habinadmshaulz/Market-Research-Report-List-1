<p><strong>The Vehicle Speed Sensor VSS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Vehicle Speed Sensor</li><li>Hall-type Vehicle Speed Sensor</li><li>Magnetic-electric Vehicle Speed Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Speed Sensor (VSS) market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Optical Vehicle Speed Sensor**: Utilizes light beams to detect wheel rotation, offering high precision and durability.</p><p>2. **Hall-type Vehicle Speed Sensor**: Employs magnetic fields to measure rotational speed, known for reliability and simplicity in installation. </p><p>3. **Magnetic-electric Vehicle Speed Sensor**: Combines magnetic and electric principles, converting magnetic field changes into electrical signals for effective speed measurement.</p><p>These sensors are essential for automotive systems, enhancing performance and safety.</p></p>
