<p><p>The spunbond polypropylene nonwovens market is divided into hydrophobic and hydrophilic types, each serving distinct applications. Hydrophobic PP nonwovens repel water, making them ideal for uses like protective clothing, medical products, and filtration materials, where moisture resistance is crucial. In contrast, hydrophilic PP nonwovens attract water, enhancing absorption, and are often utilized in hygiene products, like diapers and feminine care items, where fluid management is essential. Both types leverage the versatility of polypropylene for various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
